About ChronoSpeed

ChronoSpeed is The project I worked on for my major pproject and end of my course lasting 6 months.

For this Project I was the Programmer in charge of the the Car Physics controller, UI and AI.

Chronospeed Pushed the limit of the Car controller as the setup was orignally for a racing simulation setup. Meaning trying to convert a realistic car controller to a semi-realistic, arcady racing game gave quite a challenge.
